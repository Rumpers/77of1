## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-04-01 | Denial of Service | Sentry quota (regression spam) | mitigate | upsertJobScheduler fires exactly once/week (pattern "0 2 * * 1"); the worker emits a single captureMessage per regressing run. Well within Sentry free-tier limits (04-RESEARCH Security Domain). |
| T-04-04-02 | Tampering | eval-probe pollution of safety_audit_log on weekly runs | mitigate | The weekly run calls runEval with isRegressionRun:true, which reuses the 04-02 isolated eval-probe sessions/fanIdHash and never invokes the escalation scorer — Monday-morning runs cannot poison MOD-07 or inflate dashboards. |
